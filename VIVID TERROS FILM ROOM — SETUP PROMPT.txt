VIVID TERROS FILM ROOM — SETUP PROMPT

Copy everything below and paste it as the first message in a new Claude project:

I am a sales manager for Vivid in the Direct to Home (DTH) channel. My team uses door-to-door sales and we track our activity through an app called Terros. Each week I will upload our Terros weekly report and I need you to analyze it and build a "film room" style report — meaning I want to understand effort, efficiency, and conversion the way a coach watches game tape.

When I upload a Terros report, here is what I need you to do:

1. Read and parse the data including these key fields:

Rep name, Days Knocked, Hours Knocked, Knocks, Pitches, Leads, ACs, SRAs
Avg First Pitch Time, Avg Last Pitch Time
Days Since Last AC, Days Since Last SRA
2. Calculate derived metrics:

Pitch rate (pitches ÷ knocks)
Pitches per day (pitches ÷ days knocked)
Pitches per hour (pitches ÷ hours knocked)
Lead → AC conversion rate
AC → SRA conversion rate
3. Build a downloadable HTML one-pager reportwith the following tabs:

Overview — team KPIs (active reps, ghost reps, total pitches, knocks, ACs, SRAs, pitch rate, avg days out), top 5 by pitches, top 5 by ACs, and 3–4 key concerns for the week
Effort board — full roster ranked by pitches with days out, knocks, hours, pitch rate, pitches/day, ACs, and an effort rating (Elite / Solid / Average / Low Output / Inconsistent)
Start times — every rep's avg first and last pitch time, flagged as Early / On Time / Late / Very Late based on first pitch hour
Conversion — all reps with leads, showing the full funnel: pitches → leads → ACs → SRAs with conversion rates
Red flags — reps needing urgent attention or coaching, categorized by issue type (zero ACs despite high effort, low pitch rate, late starts, long dry spells)
Ghost reps — all reps with zero activity, split between those with prior history and those with no history, showing days since last AC/SRA
Film room — 6–8 individual rep standout cards (both positive and negative) telling the story behind the numbers
4. Design standards for the report:

Professional, clean design using dark header with white text, light body
Syne font for headings, DM Sans for body, DM Mono for labels and data
Color-coded badges: green = good, amber = needs attention, red = urgent
Inline bar charts for the effort leaderboard
Fully self-contained HTML file (no external dependencies except Google Fonts)
Mobile responsive
5. After building the report, give me a verbal summary of:

The 3 biggest things to address this week
The bright spots worth recognizing
Any rep-specific patterns worth a 1:1 conversation
Effort rating definitions:

Elite = 5+ days AND 6+ pitches per day
Solid = 4+ days AND 4+ pitches per day
Low Output = 4+ days but under 4 pitches per day
Inconsistent = 2 or fewer days out
Average = everything else
Start time flag definitions:

Early = first pitch before 2:00 PM
On Time = first pitch 2:00–4:59 PM
Late = first pitch 5:00–5:59 PM
Very Late = first pitch 6:00 PM or after
